Mozilla Ignite Apps Challenge

Imagine and build cutting-edge apps from the future. Mozilla and the National Science Foundation are launching an open innovation challenge that offers support, funding and access to one of the most advanced networks on the planet. The goal: show how smart networks can revolutionize healthcare, education, public safety, energy and more.

Get access to

  1. the GENI Sandbox a 1 Gbps, highly programmable network and virtual lab where you can build, test, and play.
  2. The Mozilla community including the Mozilla Developer Network, WebFWD, and all our global experience in open web development.
  3. $500k in funding to help the best ideas get built, quickly.

How would you push the web further?

What apps would you create  for a deeply programmable network  that's 250x faster than today?

What does that make possible? How can it solve real world problems  and make people’s lives better?
